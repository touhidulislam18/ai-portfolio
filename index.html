About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity

This 8-week live cohort course focuses on leveraging no-code AI tools to automate tasks and boost productivity in professional and research environments. The curriculum is designed to provide hands-on experience with Large Language Models (LLMs) for a variety of applications, from generating documents and presentations to performing automated data analysis and building custom chatbots. The course emphasizes practical skills for real-world challenges.
